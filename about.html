About GOM.Ai

Gathering of Minds (GOM) is a cherished morning assembly tradition at Westbridge International School of Phnom Penh, designed to reinforce our core values: responsibility, collaboration, caring and sharing, honesty, respect, reflectiveness, and open-mindedness. This GOM AI tool is a new initiative, developed by our ICT teacher in collaboration with other faculty members, to support teachers in creating engaging and meaningful GOM activities.

Powered by Gemini AI, this tool offers assistance in brainstorming ideas, suggesting themes, and outlining potential activities aligned with our core values. It is designed to be a supportive resource for teachers, recognizing their crucial role in shaping the GOM experience. While the AI can offer suggestions and inspiration, the final activity design and implementation remain entirely at the professional discretion of the teachers.

We believe this AI-powered assistance will enhance the GOM experience for our students, further nurturing their understanding and embodiment of Westbridge's core values.

Important Note: This tool is intended solely to support Westbridge International School teachers in developing GOM activities. No copyright infringement is intended.
