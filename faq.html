Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)


Q: What is Gathering of Minds (GOM)?

A: Gathering of Minds is a daily morning assembly at Westbridge International School of Phnom Penh. It's a vital part of our school culture, focused on reinforcing our core values: responsibility, collaboration, caring and sharing, honesty, respect, reflectiveness, and open-mindedness.


Q: What is the purpose of the GOM AI?

A: The GOM AI is designed to assist teachers in creating engaging and impactful GOM activities. It provides suggestions, ideas, and frameworks based on Westbridge's core values, helping teachers plan and execute meaningful assemblies.


Q: Who developed the GOM AI?

A: The GOM AI was developed by the ICT teacher at Westbridge International School of Phnom Penh in collaboration with other teachers.


Q: How does the GOM AI work?

A: The GOM AI, powered by Gemini AI, uses its capabilities to generate ideas and suggestions for GOM activities based on the input provided by the teachers. It can help with brainstorming themes, outlining activities, and ensuring alignment with Westbridge's core values.


Q: Does the GOM AI replace the teachers' role in planning GOM activities?

A: Absolutely not. The GOM AI is a support tool only. The professional judgment and expertise of the teachers are essential in selecting, adapting, and implementing the final GOM activity. The AI is meant to be a helpful assistant, not a replacement for teacher creativity and experience.


Q: Is the GOM AI available to anyone?

A: No, the GOM AI is specifically designed for and accessible only to Westbridge International School of Phnom Penh teachers for the purpose of developing GOM activities.


Q: Is there any copyright issue with the GOM AI?

A: No copyright infringement is intended. The GOM AI is developed specifically for Westbridge International School. All logos, materials, and intellectual property associated with Westbridge International School remain the sole property of the school.
